PitWall is evolving into a motorsport intelligence platform for Formula 1 and MotoGP — combining race weekends, live event tracking, strategy analysis, and driver intelligence into a single, editorial-grade experience.

## Development Principles

PitWall is built around a small set of engineering principles:

- Build reusable engines instead of isolated features.
- Keep deterministic logic separate from presentation.
- Prefer modular architecture.
- Keep Formula 1 and MotoGP on a shared platform.
- Prioritise maintainability and scalability.
